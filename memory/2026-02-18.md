## Project: DotSpeak
Two products so far, same stack: Python, FastAPI, Claude API (sonnet), SQLite, vanilla HTML/CSS/JS

### Sympathy Dot
- Sender answers 4 guided questions about someone who passed
- Q1: personality/quirks, Q2: one perfect story, Q3: catchphrase/sayings, Q4: what receiver needs to hear
- Claude extracts persona, creates unique chat link
- Receiver chats with warm AI that unfolds the 4 answers naturally — teases story, drops catchphrase organically
- Dark purple theme, modern/jazzy but sad
- AssemblyAI for voice transcription (free tier, no LeMUR)
- Tested working end-to-end

### Powwow Dot
- Group planning chat (2-12 people) with an AI participant
- Creator sets topic + event date, gets shareable link (WhatsApp share button)
- WebSocket real-time group chat
- Dot does: background research on topic, decision tracking, open question surfacing
- Status panel with decisions/questions/research
- Dot responds intelligently (not every message) — tracks [DECISION:] [QUESTION:] [RESOLVED:] tags
